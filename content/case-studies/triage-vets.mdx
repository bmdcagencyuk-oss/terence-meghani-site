## (01) The Challenge

Veterinary decisions are emotional rather than economic — pet owners choose a practice on first impression, often long before they ever meet a clinician. Triage Vets, an independently-owned, RCVS-accredited veterinary practice in Mill Hill, NW7 London, had the clinical credentials, the bedside manner and the technical depth, but a brand presence that was largely indistinguishable from hundreds of other local practices across North London. The challenge was to make the calibre of the clinic visible from the first scroll of a search result.

The remit covered everything client-facing: a full veterinary brand identity, a new website built around real-world booking and emergency contact patterns, commissioned photography that reflected the actual clinic and its team, and a marketing framework the clinic's in-house team could run independently after handover. The work needed to project trust and professionalism without sliding into the corporate cosmetic gloss that defines the larger national veterinary chains the practice competes against.

## (02) The Approach

The identity was designed to signal trust before a visitor reads the first line of copy. A confident wordmark, a restrained three-colour palette, and a typography system chosen for legibility across signage, forms, prescription labels and screen — workhorses that hold up across every client touchpoint a veterinary practice produces. Brand guidelines documented the system for the team's internal use across signage, stationery, digital and print.

No stock imagery was used anywhere in the project. Every photograph on the site was commissioned, featuring real staff, real patients and real treatment rooms inside the Mill Hill clinic. The website was rebuilt with practical utility in mind: booking and emergency contact accessible within two taps on mobile, service descriptions written in plain English, and transparent information about what an appointment actually involves. Marketing support included Google Business Profile optimisation, a review capture workflow tuned to the practice's day-to-day operations, and local SEO content aligned with the NW7 catchment area.

## (03) The Outcome

A complete veterinary brand identity system was delivered across signage, digital, print and clinical collateral, with the new website live at triagevets.com and a full commissioned photography library licensed to the practice in perpetuity. The marketing framework was handed over with documentation, so that the clinic's own front-of-house and management team can maintain Google Business Profile activity, review capture and local SEO content without an ongoing retainer.

The combined work has given Triage Vets a digital presence that holds its own against the national chains active across North London while remaining recognisably independent and locally owned. The brand system continues to serve the practice across patient onboarding, signage, print collateral and digital marketing, and the website acts as the primary inbound channel for new patient registrations across the Mill Hill catchment.
