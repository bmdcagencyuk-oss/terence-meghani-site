## (01) The Challenge

Commercial cleaning is a B2B category where buyers want clarity before they enquire. Procurement teams, office managers and construction site managers all arrive on a supplier's website looking for quick, unambiguous answers: exactly which services are offered, in which contexts, at what scale, and to what standard. Anything that requires the buyer to dig is friction that costs Kemp Services enquiries to competitors with clearer presentation, regardless of how strong the underlying operation actually is.

Kemp Services — specialising in contract cleaning, office cleaning and builders' cleaning — needed a website that reflected the professionalism of the operation and made the service offering navigable at a glance. The brief was tightly focused on web rather than a wider brand or marketing programme, but the work still had to do meaningful commercial lifting: turn the site from a passive brochure into an active enquiry channel for the kind of B2B buyers who decide quickly and rarely come back to compare a second time.

## (02) The Approach

The website was redeveloped with a clean, modern design aligned to Kemp's professional ethos and to the practical decision-making patterns of commercial cleaning buyers. Each of the core service lines — contract cleaning, office cleaning and builders' cleaning — was given its own detailed section, written so that prospective clients could immediately understand which offering fits their requirement, what it covers, and what engagement looks like in practice. Service descriptions were treated as decision-support content rather than marketing copy.

Responsive design was prioritised throughout, ensuring parity across desktop, tablet and mobile, since procurement and facilities buyers genuinely use all three at different points in the research process. Page weight was kept low for fast loading, navigation was kept minimal, and the structure was built for clarity over embellishment — no decorative content, no padding, no friction between landing on the site and submitting an enquiry. The site was developed to be straightforward for the in-house team to update without ongoing development support.

## (03) The Outcome

A new commercial cleaning website was delivered, structured around the three core service lines and built to act as the primary inbound enquiry channel for the business. The site replaced an underperforming digital presence with a clear, fast, mobile-friendly platform that lets B2B buyers — facilities managers, office managers, construction site managers — get the information they need and contact the team without unnecessary work.

The site was handed over with documentation so that day-to-day content updates can be made by the Kemp Services team without requiring further development input. The combined work has continued to serve the business as a working commercial tool — turning research traffic into qualified enquiries from the kinds of customers Kemp's contract, office and builders' cleaning services are built for.
