## (01) The Challenge

High-street laundry and dry-cleaning is a category defined by local loyalty and almost-invisible digital presence. Most competing local services businesses rely entirely on walk-in footfall, with websites — where they exist at all — that are outdated, mobile-unfriendly, and rarely updated beyond an opening-hours block. For a category where customers genuinely do search "dry cleaners near me" before stepping out of the house, that gap between in-store quality and online presence quietly costs trade every week.

Metro Laundrette & Dry Cleaners came in with an established walk-in customer base and high in-store standards across laundry, dry-cleaning, alterations and specialist garment care, but no digital footprint to match. The brief covered the full local services package: brand refresh, a new website, commissioned photography and a marketing framework calibrated to the realistic search and review behaviour of a high-street laundry catchment.

## (02) The Approach

The identity was refreshed to bring consistency across signage, packaging, in-store collateral and digital, giving the business a single coherent visual treatment from shopfront to social profile. The new website was built mobile-first, since the dominant mode of use for a local laundry and dry-cleaning service is a phone search from home — services, transparent pricing, opening hours, location and contact information were all surfaced without friction, with the structure favouring clarity over decorative content.

Local SEO was implemented to capture search intent from the surrounding catchment, with on-page optimisation aligned to the searches customers actually run for dry-cleaning, ironing services and specialist garment care. Google Business Profile was tightened and a review capture workflow was set up so that ongoing reviews feed back into local search visibility. Commissioned photography replaced stock imagery across the site and printed collateral, showing the actual shop, equipment and team rather than category-stock visuals.

## (03) The Outcome

A complete brand refresh was delivered alongside a new mobile-first website, a commissioned photography library and a structured local marketing framework covering Google Business Profile, on-page SEO, review capture and supporting social content. The combined work moved Metro from invisibility on local search to a coherent, discoverable high-street laundry and dry-cleaning brand with a digital presence consistent with the in-store experience.

The system was handed over with documentation so that the in-house team can maintain it without an ongoing retainer — updating service information, capturing reviews, posting seasonal content and refreshing photography as the business evolves. The website continues to act as the primary inbound channel for new customers from the local catchment, and the brand refresh has settled into the everyday operation of the business across signage, packaging and print.
