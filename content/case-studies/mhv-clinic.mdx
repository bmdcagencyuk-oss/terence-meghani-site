## (01) The Challenge

Wellness and healthcare clinics live or die on trust and discoverability. Prospective patients arrive almost entirely from search — researching a specific concern, comparing local providers, judging credibility from the calibre of the website inside the first few seconds. MHV Clinic needed a strong digital presence aligned with its mission to support individuals toward total wellness, and a website that would perform reliably on every device a prospective patient might use to find it.

The brief covered both the digital build and the inbound demand layer. The clinic needed a fast, modern, mobile-first website that could carry the weight of detailed service content without becoming heavy or hard to navigate, alongside a paid search programme calibrated to the specific intent-driven queries that wellness patients actually run. The combined work had to convert research traffic into booked appointments without compromising on the considered, professional tone the category demands.

## (02) The Approach

The new website was built for speed, clarity and mobile-first usability. Every element — content structure, image weight, page load, navigation, accessibility — was tuned for the phone-based research behaviour that dominates wellness-clinic enquiry, giving prospective patients a frictionless path from initial research through to the contact and booking points. Service pages were structured to answer the questions a patient arrives with, written in plain English, and supported by a navigation hierarchy that surfaces the most important information without scrolling work.

A paid search programme ran in parallel to capture intent-based traffic from prospective patients actively searching for wellness, healthcare and treatment-specific services. Campaigns were structured by service line and by intent, with budget concentrated against the queries most likely to convert, and landing pages aligned to the paid creative so that ad-clickers landed on the precise content they were promised. Conversion tracking was set up so that the clinic team could see clearly which campaigns were producing booked appointments versus passing traffic.

## (03) The Outcome

A new wellness clinic website was delivered live, supported by a structured paid search programme covering the clinic's core service lines and a set of service-specific landing pages tied to the campaigns. The combined system gives MHV Clinic a coherent digital presence — discoverable in the searches that matter, fast and trustworthy on the device most patients use, and configured so that paid spend feeds measurable patient enquiries rather than untracked traffic.

The work was handed over with documentation and dashboards so that the in-house team can monitor campaign performance and continue to operate the site without an open-ended retainer. The website continues to serve as the clinic's primary inbound channel, and the paid search activity remains the most direct lever for new patient acquisition — supporting sustained enquiry volume from the catchment the clinic serves.
